About me

I am a software engineer at Red Hat Integration, working mostly on Apache Camel Quarkus.

Earlier, I used to be a sustaining engineer for WildFly Camel (the upstream of Fuse on EAP), JBoss EAP/WildFly and other Red Hat Middleware projects.

I like tinkering around build tools, most notably mvnd (a.k.a. Maven daemon), srcdeps (source dependencies for Maven and Gradle) and ec4j (EditorConfig for Java).

I speak on software conferences and Java user groups from time to time.
